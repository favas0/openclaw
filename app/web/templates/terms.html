Terms of Service

OpenClaw terms of service

OpenClaw is provided as a self-hosted research and review tool. It is intended for lawful marketplace analysis, internal evaluation, and operator decision support.

Permitted use

  • Use OpenClaw to collect, evaluate, and report on marketplace product signals.
  • Use the web shell to support provider review, compliance checks, and OAuth integration flows.
  • Operate the service only with credentials, data access, and marketplace permissions you are authorized to use.

Restrictions

  • Do not use OpenClaw to impersonate a marketplace, storefront, or consumer checkout flow.
  • Do not use OpenClaw for unlawful scraping, credential abuse, or policy-violating automation.
  • Do not rely on generated research output as a substitute for legal, financial, or compliance advice.

Operational responsibility

  • The operator remains responsible for deployment security, access control, data retention, and provider compliance.
  • The CLI pipeline remains the primary workflow for ingestion, scoring, and exports.
  • The web shell is intentionally thin and may expose reviewer information from the existing database when available.
