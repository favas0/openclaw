Privacy Policy

OpenClaw privacy policy

OpenClaw is a self-hosted research tool. It is intended for marketplace analysis, not for consumer ordering, account resale, or storefront operations.

Information processed

  • Configuration provided by the operator, including API credentials and local runtime settings.
  • Marketplace listing data collected through approved or demo integrations for research purposes.
  • Generated research outputs such as clustered opportunities, rankings, reports, and review exports.
  • OAuth callback query parameters such as code, state, and provider errors when an integration flow redirects back to OpenClaw.

How data is used

  • To analyze marketplace product opportunities and generate operator-facing research outputs.
  • To validate marketplace integrations and support provider review or compliance flows.
  • To troubleshoot connectivity, health, and local deployment issues.

Storage model

  • OpenClaw stores operational data locally in SQLite under the operator-managed data directory.
  • LLM enrichment is designed for local Ollama usage rather than a required hosted AI service.
  • Operators control hosting, retention, backups, and deployment access.
