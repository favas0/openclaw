Approval-Friendly Web Shell

OpenClaw is a self-hosted marketplace research agent for product discovery and operator review.

OpenClaw helps operators collect marketplace listings, normalize them, cluster similar products, enrich clusters with local model output, score opportunities with deterministic logic, and export review packs. The core workflow remains the Python CLI; this web layer is a thin surface for website legitimacy, policy pages, OAuth callbacks, and reviewer access.

Web pages + callbacks
Privacy, terms, support, health, reviewer view, Etsy callback, and TikTok callback endpoints.

What OpenClaw does

Marketplace research

  • Collects source-specific marketplace listing data.
  • Normalizes noisy listing titles into comparable product signals.
  • Clusters overlapping listings into product opportunities.

Deterministic evaluation

  • Scores demand, competition, supplier fit, risk, economics, and review signals.
  • Stores outputs in SQLite for repeatable local operation.
  • Exports ranked products, shortlists, trend reports, and review packs.

Approval and compliance surface

  • Public-facing homepage and product description.
  • Privacy policy, terms, and support pages.
  • Dedicated OAuth callback URLs for Etsy and TikTok review flows.
